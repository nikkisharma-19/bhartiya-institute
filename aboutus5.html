Vice Chairman's Message

Col. P.S. Bhatnagar(Retd.)

(Chief Advisor)

Education is the bedrock of any civilisation.The quality of citizens in any country depends on the quality of Education provided to its students-the youth of the nation. All round development of any Nation largely depends upon its youth.

God Almighty has blessed every individual with immense potential and every human being is a potential god. No report card/Mark sheet of any student can ever measure the true potential of any student, a student may be very weak in Mathematics, but he may be excellent in Physics/Chemistry or Biology or any other subject. The education and total quality education helps us to realize our true potential and it is here when the true role of Bhartiya Institute of Engineering and Technology begins. Here every student is given an equal opportunity to realise his/her true potential.

Have faith on yourself, have faith on God almighty,be sincere and truthful to yourself, be self disclipned and the success shall be at your door step. This is the only secret of success which I have revealed it to you in few words.

I on behalf of entire team of Bhartiya Institute of Engineering and Technology wish you all the very best for your very bright future ahead.
